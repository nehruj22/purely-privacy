Go to Settings → Account 2. Tap "Delete Account" 3. Confirm deletion 4. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days Data Retention: • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active • Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days • Legal requirements: Some data may be retained if required by law --- CHILDREN'S PRIVACY Purely is strictly for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover an underage user, we will immediately delete their account and data. --- LOCATION DATA We do not share precise location. Location is displayed at the city level only.
