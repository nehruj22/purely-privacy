Purely Community Guidelines

Last updated:

Purely is a sober dating community. We want it to feel safe, respectful, and real. If you use Purely, you agree to follow these rules.

1) Be respectful

  • No harassment, hate speech, threats, bullying, or doxxing.
  • No sexual exploitation, coercion, or unwanted sexual content.
  • No discrimination based on race, religion, nationality, disability, gender identity, sexual orientation, or age.

2) Be authentic

  • Use your real identity (no impersonation).
  • No misleading profiles, catfishing, or deceptive intent.
  • No spam, scams, pyramid schemes, or solicitation.

3) Safety first

  • Don’t share private info publicly (address, banking details, passwords, etc.).
  • If you meet in person, choose public places and tell a friend.
  • If someone asks for money or gifts, report them immediately.

4) Content rules

  • No explicit sexual content or pornography.
  • No illegal content, buying/selling drugs, or instructions to do harm.
  • No violent or graphic content.

5) Sober community expectations

  • Respect each person’s sobriety journey and boundaries.
  • Do not pressure others to drink or use substances.

6) Reporting and enforcement

We may remove content, limit features, suspend, or ban accounts that violate these guidelines.

Report issues in-app (when available) or contact support using the Support page.
